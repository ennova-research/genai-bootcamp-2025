## ğŸ§± Generative AI: Core Idea

<!-- > AI models that *create* new content: text, images, code, music, and more. -->
> Train DL models to *re-generate* existing information: hide a part of it, learn to predict what's missing.

- For text: given first $n$ words, predict the word $n+1$.
- For images: given image at resolution $n$ (blurred), predict how it looks like at resolution $n+1$ (not so blurred).
